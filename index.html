Banking to Bricklynn: A Culinary Journey


Nilay Chowdhury’s journey from banking to founding Bricklynn is a story of passion and resilience. Leaving behind a stable banking career, Nilay pursued his dream of creating something unique — a cozy QSR where quality meets affordability. With Bricklynn, he aimed to make delicious food accessible to everyone, blending the warmth of a café with the efficiency of a QSR.

Despite challenges in the food industry, Nilay’s financial acumen helped him navigate obstacles creatively. His winning formula? Quality food made with love, pocket-friendly prices, a welcoming ambiance, and smart social media marketing.

Beyond his own success, Nilay mentors aspiring entrepreneurs, hoping to inspire others to chase their dreams. Looking forward, he envisions expanding Bricklynn nationwide and introducing new, customer-inspired dishes. “Food is love, and love is food. I'm just spreading that love, one meal at a time,” Nilay reflects.

The Pepperoni Magnifico

Dough (flour, water, yeast, salt, sugar), sauce (crushed tomatoes, garlic, oregano, basil, salt, pepper), pepperoni slices, mozzarella, parmesan, red pepper flakes, Italian herbs.

Bold and brimming with flavour, the Pepperoni Magnifico brings together the finest, zesty pepperoni atop a rich layer of melted cheese. Each slice offers a harmony of spice and savoury goodness, capturing the spirit of traditional Italian recipes and modern indulgence. Perfectly crisped and seasoned to perfection, it’s a culinary masterpiece for true pizza lovers who crave an unforgettable bite and an explosion of flavours with every mouthful.

The Tikki Temptation

Soft burger buns, chicken tikki patty (ground chicken, garlic, ginger, green chilies, coriander powder, cumin, garam masala, breadcrumbs, salt, pepper, egg), spicy mayo, fresh herbs, gherkin.

A juicy, seasoned chicken tikki burger inspired by the flavours of Indian street food. Nestled in a soft bun with fresh herbs and a touch of spicy mayo, The Tikki Temptation balances fiery and creamy elements for a bold and delicious bite. Each mouthful brings a taste of India’s vibrant food culture with an indulgent twist, showcasing a medley of spices that create a symphony of flavours, making it an unforgettable experience for all burger enthusiasts.

Greek Meats Grill

Pita bread, grilled chicken, tomato slices, crumbled feta, cucumber, yogurt-based tzatziki sauce (yogurt, cucumber, garlic, ghirkin), herbs.

A fresh take on Mediterranean classics, the Greek Meats Grill combines juicy grilled meats with tomatoes, crumbled feta, and a hint of spices for rich flavour. Wrapped in soft pita bread, it captures the essence of Greek flavours, bringing warmth and zest to each bite. It’s a delicious journey to the Mediterranean with a satisfying twist that tantalizes the taste buds, showcasing a delightful harmony of textures and aromas that elevate your dining experience. Perfect for those who are craving comfort food with a Mediterranean flair, this dish will leave you wanting more.

Fiery Phoenix Wings

Chicken wings, marinade (red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, coriander powder, garlic paste, ginger paste, salt, lemon juice), garnish (fresh coriander, green chilies).

Seasoned with a blend of bold Indian masalas, the Fiery Phoenix Wings pack a serious punch. Each wing is coated in a smoky, spicy marinade that infuses them with depth and richness, perfect for spice lovers. This fiery flavour bomb celebrates Indian spices, leaving you craving more heat in every bite and delivering an unforgettable experience that ignites your passion for bold flavours. Ideal for sharing with friends or enjoying solo, these wings are sure to be a favorite at any gathering.

The K-Drama Fries

Crispy potato fries, Flour, seasoning (salt, pepper, paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, peri peri powder, thyme and oregano), dipping sauce (mayo, garlic, pepper).

Named in honor of Korean drama, these fries are crispy and golden, seasoned and prepared to perfection. Each bite offers a satisfying crunch that adds flair to any meal, capturing the excitement of bold, comforting flavours. These are fries that bring a little drama to your day, with every delicious serving, creating a delightful experience that pairs perfectly with your favorite dips. Whether enjoyed as a snack, side, or main attraction, they transform ordinary moments into extraordinary ones, making every occasion feel special and unforgettable.

Tropical Pineapple Cream

Pineapple syrup, Milk, Whipping Cream

Indulge in the Tropical Pineapple Cream, a delightful dessert that captures the essence of a sun-soaked paradise. This refreshing treat features luscious pineapple syrup, expertly blended with creamy milk and whipped to perfection, resulting in an irresistibly smooth texture. Each spoonful delivers a perfect balance of sweetness and tang, offering a rich and frosty escape that tantalizes your taste buds. Perfectly suited for warm days or any occasion, this delightful dessert transports you to a tropical oasis, making every bite a blissful experience that leaves you craving more.
